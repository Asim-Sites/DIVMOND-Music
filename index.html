About DIVMOND

DIVMOND (pronounced Diamond) is Chicago-based singer/songwriter/producer. Born in Houston and raised in the suburbs of the Windy City, DIVMOND has been making music for over a decade. His sound draws influences from hip-hop, jazz, R&B, and spoken word/poetry; his strength and versatility as a vocalist shines through in each track.

DIVMOND is establishing himself as a force in the local music community, motivating and inspiring peers and the generations ahead. He’s an alumnus of Louder Than A Bomb, the world's largest youth poetry festival, and an active member of Sofar Sounds. A natural collaborator, he jumps at the opportunity to broaden his range. DIVMOND's late 2017 collaboration with producer duo, Cafe Disko garnered one million streams across all platforms.

DIVMOND’s latest music comes on the heels of a 5-year hiatus. "I took a break from creating or performing because I wanted to—as cheesy as it sounds—become a better person. I got to the point where I felt like what was coming out creatively was the closest to sincerity, the closest to my core.”

"I find that a lot of my new music is declarative, confessional. I write most of my new music for my friends and for my other relationships.”
